PART A - D3 Programming

Q. What is the relationship between age and average salary of data scientists?

Note: Scatter plot is extremely useful in finding correlations between the variables( Age & avg. Salaries in this case.) Thus, we will be applying the graph using age as x-axis and avg. salary as y-axis to conduct the analysis.

Data Cleaning: This dataset contains ages ranging from -1 to 234 and unrelated careers. We will only be selecting age between 18 and 100 as our critical data as range outside are extreme values that we considered unlikely happen in our life, as well as taking unrelated and invalid careers(such as director and NAs) out of the research.

Plot:

Findings to the visualization

The graph shown that there is a positive relationship between age and average salary of being a data scientist. The positive trend gives the proof.
In addition, we also observed that data scientists have a higher mean salary on average comparing to the other related field, such as data engineers, data modeler, and analysts. Careers such as data analysts and data engineer even has a negative trend towards relationship between age and average salary.
